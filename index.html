About Maeve

Maeve is one of many offensive flanks in Paladins. She can be regarded as being one of the strongest flanks in Paladins! She dual wields daggers which pierce through the armour of damage dealers and support class making her an excellent team player!

Maeve and her piercing daggers

Maeve's Champion Traits

  • She's exteremely agile
  • She's overpowered
  • She can blind enemies

Champion Abilities

Maeves abilities allow here to be agile in the battlefield whilst dealing killing blows each time. By pressing Q, maeves abilities cooldown resets and she can use all her other abilities again. This makes her extremely hard to go against. These are her abilities:

  1. Daggers
  2. Pounce
  3. Nine Lives
  4. Prowl
  5. Midnight (Ultimate)
